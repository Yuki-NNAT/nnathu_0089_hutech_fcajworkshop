### General Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment throughout the internship was professional and disciplined while remaining friendly and collaborative. Team members were open, approachable, and always willing to support one another. During working hours, everyone focused seriously on their assigned tasks, creating a suitable environment for studying technical documentation, configuring systems, and completing project-related work.

However, working at the office required prior registration and approval. Therefore, the number of opportunities I had to work directly with other team members was relatively limited.

**2. Support from Mentors and Team Administrators**  
The mentors and team administrators provided enthusiastic and dedicated support throughout my internship. Whenever I encountered technical issues that I did not fully understand, they patiently explained the concepts, provided guidance, and supported me step by step until the problems became clear.

During the final project, they regularly monitored my progress, offered constructive feedback, and created favorable conditions for me to complete my assigned responsibilities. Even when I needed assistance outside regular working hours, they still made an effort to respond and provide the necessary guidance. I greatly appreciate the time, knowledge, and dedication they shared with me throughout the internship.

**3. Relevance of the Work to My Field of Study**  
The internship content was highly relevant to my Information Technology major and career orientation. Through practical exercises and the final project, I had the opportunity to apply the knowledge acquired at university to building, deploying, and managing a system on a cloud computing platform.

In particular, while participating in the **AI Recipe Finder** project, I directly performed tasks related to data processing, database design, Backend API development, and system deployment using AWS services such as Amazon RDS, Amazon EC2, and Amazon Cognito. This experience helped me better understand the differences between theoretical knowledge and the practical implementation of a real-world system.

**4. Learning and Skill Development Opportunities**  
The internship helped me improve both my technical knowledge and professional mindset. I had the opportunity to gain a deeper understanding of the process of developing and deploying an application in a Cloud environment, including database design, Backend development, server configuration, and user authentication.

In addition to technical skills, I also improved my ability to independently study documentation, analyze and troubleshoot issues, write technical reports, manage progress, and collaborate with team members. The knowledge and experience gained during the internship also helped me better understand my abilities, career direction, and the skills I need to continue developing in the future.

**5. Team Culture and Teamwork**  
Team members worked together with equality, mutual respect, and a willingness to support one another. Although I was an intern, my opinions were still heard, and I had opportunities to present ideas, discuss solutions, and contribute to the team’s shared work.

When difficult problems arose or tasks needed to be completed within a limited timeframe, everyone was willing to discuss them together and identify appropriate solutions. This made me feel like a genuine member of the team and motivated me to fulfill my responsibilities effectively.

**6. Policies and Benefits for Interns**  
The program provided favorable conditions for students to balance their internship schedules with their academic responsibilities. Flexible working arrangements allowed me to complete my assigned tasks while maintaining progress on university coursework and reports.

The support provided to interns was also appropriate and demonstrated recognition of the time, effort, and contributions made by students throughout the program.

### Additional Questions

- What were you **most satisfied with** during the internship?  

What satisfied me most was the opportunity to learn and work in a serious, professional, and highly disciplined environment. I directly participated in developing a real-world project, applied AWS services, and received dedicated guidance from the mentors. Their support helped me become more confident when approaching new knowledge and technologies.

- What do you think the company **could improve** for future interns?  

The company could consider streamlining the registration and approval process for working at the office, allowing interns to spend more time working directly with mentors and other team members. Increasing the number of on-site working sessions would improve communication, technical support, and knowledge sharing.

- Would you **recommend this internship to your friends**? Why?  

Yes. I would particularly recommend it to students interested in Cloud Computing and AWS who wish to work in a professional, focused, and disciplined environment. The program provides valuable practical experience, exposure to AWS services, and opportunities to develop technical thinking under the dedicated guidance of experienced mentors.

---

### Suggestions and Expectations

- Do you have any suggestions for improving the internship experience?  

I hope the office schedule registration process can be completed more quickly or that the program can arrange additional on-site working sessions. This would enable interns to interact more frequently with mentors, strengthen connections with team members, and improve learning effectiveness.

In addition, the program could organize more in-depth knowledge-sharing sessions, technical evaluations, or practical experience discussions tailored to different career paths. These activities would help interns better understand the expectations of a professional working environment.

- Would you like to continue participating in this program in the future?  

I would greatly appreciate the opportunity to continue participating in the program through more advanced and challenging projects. If a suitable opportunity becomes available, I would also like to become an official member, continue developing my professional capabilities, and make long-term contributions to the organization.
